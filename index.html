Graph Information

This interactive graph presents the Gut Microbiome Constellation, depicting the complex network of species in the human gut. Each point (node) represents a distinct microorganism, and the lines (edges) illustrate direct relationships between them. These connections, revealed using the Graphical Lasso algorithm, highlight significant interactions within the microbiome.
